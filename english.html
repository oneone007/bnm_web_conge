The Listening Script
Emma: Jack! What do you do every day?
Jack: Well, I always wake up at 7 o'clock. First, I get ready for school and then I have my breakfast.
Emma: Me too! But I sometimes wake up late. After that, I eat breakfast. I always eat toast. I don't like milk. I prefer drinking juice. What about you?
Jack: I always have cereals with milk. After that, I pack my school bag and ride my bike to school.
Emma: I never go to school by bus. I enjoy walking. It's fun!
Jack: Nice! Maybe I will walk sometimes, too!

Script

Emma: Hi! I'm Emma from London.

I always wake up at 7:00 and eat breakfast.

Then I go to school. At lunch, I usually share sandwiches with my friend.

In the afternoon, I return home and do my homework. After that, I watch TV.

At 9:00, I have dinner with my family, brush my teeth and write in my diary.

I can't skip this habit. Finally, I go to bed.

On weekends sometimes I go to the park with my family.
